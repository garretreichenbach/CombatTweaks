# CombatTweaks

A StarMade mod that adds real-time, RTS-style tactical fleet command and a passive armor HP defense layer. Open a tactical overlay of your sector, select and command ships (and individual turrets), read the battlefield at a glance, and build with armor that actually soaks damage.

## Overview

CombatTweaks brings bird's-eye fleet command to StarMade without leaving your cockpit. Press the tactical-map key to survey your sector, select ships, and issue orders through a radial menu — while a sector grid, status rings, incoming-contact warnings, and order paths keep you informed.

### Key Features

- **Tactical Map** — Real-time sector overlay with faction-colored markers, a sector/subsector grid, status rings (reactor/armor/shield), labels (name, faction, mass, speed, distance, sector, current order), and animated order paths.
- **Fleet AI Orders** — Command ships to attack, defend, move, mine, or repair via a radial menu, with order queueing.
- **Turret Command** — A dedicated turret mode that shows each turret as a single unit and lets you command turrets directly; orders cascade to the turret's guns with no fleet setup required.
- **Incoming Signatures** — Early-warning travel lines and labels for ships approaching or jumping in from nearby sectors, with mass/relation estimates whose accuracy depends on range and recon.
- **Selection Panel** — A pinnable, scrollable fleet status list with shield/armor/reactor bars and live order readouts.
- **In-Map Settings** — A top-left panel of toggles (status rings, label detail, signatures, grid, and more) that persists between sessions.
- **Armor HP System** — A shared armor durability pool that absorbs damage before it reaches your reactor, with diminishing bleed-through as armor is lost and tunable absorption chambers.
